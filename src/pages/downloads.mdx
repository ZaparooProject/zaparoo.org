Zaparoo requires [Zaparoo Core](#zaparoo-core) installed on your target device,
plus a frontend app such as [Zaparoo App](#zaparoo-app) to manage it and create
tokens like NFC tags. See the [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started)
guide for more detailed steps on getting up and running.

## Zaparoo App

[Zaparoo App](/docs/app) is the primary
supported frontend for Zaparoo. Manage the Zaparoo Core service, take advantage
of your phone's NFC features to easily create NFC tags and use your phone as an
NFC reader or QR code scanner. All essential app features are available for free.

If you don't want to or can't use your phone, other frontends are available like
the built-in [web UI](/docs/core/web-ui), terminal UI, [CLI](/docs/core/cli) and [API](/docs/core/api/) interfaces. Zaparoo is an open
platform that encourages useful and unique ways to access media!

## Zaparoo Core

The [Zaparoo Core](/docs/core) application
runs in the background on your device and coordinates all hardware and software
to make Zaparoo work. The service is built for minimal resource usage, so it
won't interfere with your media playing.
