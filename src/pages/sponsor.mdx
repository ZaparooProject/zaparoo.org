## Why Support Matters

Your support enables:

- **Active Development**: New features, bug fixes, and platform support
- **Infrastructure**: Hosting for websites, services, and community resources
- **Community Support**: Maintaining documentation, Discord, and helping users
- **Long-term Sustainability**: Ensuring Zaparoo stays maintained and improved

## For Businesses

If your business or venue uses Zaparoo commercially, please consider sponsoring the project. Not only does it ensure the software you rely on stays actively maintained, but it also demonstrates your commitment to supporting open-source software.

For commercial licensing and trademark inquiries, please see our [commercial use information](/contact#commercial-and-trademark-use).

A huge thank you to everyone who has supported Zaparoo through purchases, sponsorships, contributions, and community participation. This project exists because of you.
