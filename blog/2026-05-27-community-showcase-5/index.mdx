Hey! Welcome back to the community showcase. I've got another batch of awesome stuff from our [Discord](https://zaparoo.org/discord) members to share. This one has custom MiSTer cases, floppy disk launches, card back templates, cassette inserts, and a few different ways to make Zaparoo feel more like a physical game library.

## wonderloid3455

<Gallery captions={false} media={[

wonderloid3455 put together a cassette case insert and matching NFC card using [Zaparoo Designer](/docs/designer/). It’s a small one-card example, but it shows how the tools can turn a plain card and case into the start of a real collection.
