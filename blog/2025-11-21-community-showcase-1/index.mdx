## Community Projects

I love seeing new software projects that work with Zaparoo. Here's a couple that are somewhat related for generating card labels. These work a bit differently to the [Designer](https://design.zaparoo.com/), they generate labels in bulk using your existing scraped metadata.

### ZaparooSkraper

[AchillesPDX](https://github.com/AchillesPDX) has made a custom Mix for [Skraper](https://www.skraper.net/) that generates card labels.

[ZaparooSkraper on GitHub](https://github.com/AchillesPDX/ZaparooSkraper)

### zaparoo-helpers

[Thwonp](https://github.com/thwonp) put together some bash scripts that make it easier to compose and print custom cards.

[zaparoo-helpers on GitHub](https://github.com/thwonp/zaparoo-helpers)
